Case Studies

Majestic Grace Embassy

The Challenge

Majestic Grace Embassy needed a digital platform to connect with their congregation online, offering easy access to sermons, events, and donation options. Their previous website was outdated, lacked mobile responsiveness, and had poor user engagement.

The Solution

I designed and developed a modern Bootstrap-based website, integrating features like a sermon library, event calendar, and secure donation system using Stripe. I optimized the site for SEO and ensured full mobile responsiveness, using Tailwind CSS for a sleek, user-friendly design.

The Results

Within 2 months of launch, the website saw a 40% increase in online engagement, with sermon views up by 60% and online donations increasing by 25%. The site now ranks on the first page of Google for key local search terms.

NjunkimLegal

The Challenge

Njunkim Legal required a CRM platform to manage cases, communicate with clients, and streamline operations. Their existing system was manual, leading to inefficiencies and delayed responses.

The Solution

I built a custom legal CRM using PHP, MySQL, and JavaScript, with a responsive frontend designed in Tailwind CSS. The platform includes features like case tracking, client portals, automated notifications, and secure document storage. I also implemented robust cybersecurity measures to protect sensitive data.

The Results

The CRM reduced case management time by 35%, improved client response times by 50%, and increased overall firm productivity. Client satisfaction scores rose by 20% due to the improved communication features.
